About GoGreen

Engineering Sustainable Alternatives

At GoGreen, we are reinventing structural panels. Traditional plywood contributes significantly to global deforestation, taking decades for trees to mature. We looked for a better way and found it in bamboo.

Our state-of-the-art processing transforms raw bamboo stalks into high-tensile, beautiful plywood sheets perfect for architectural, furniture, and industrial design. We combine traditional craftsmanship with eco-forward technology.

Why Bamboo Plywood?

🚀

Rapidly Renewable

Unlike traditional trees that take 30+ years to grow, bamboo reaches maturity in just 3 to 5 years, making it incredibly sustainable.

💪

Superior Strength

Bamboo boasts a higher tensile strength than steel and withstands warping better than traditional hardwood plywood.

🌱

Eco-Friendly Glue

We use ultra-low emission, non-toxic adhesives to ensure that our structural panels remain clean and safe for indoor environments.
